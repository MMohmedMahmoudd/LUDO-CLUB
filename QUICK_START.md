# 🎮 LUDO Club - Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 What's New

### 1. **7 Different Token Shapes**
Before starting a game, choose how your tokens look:
- 🔵 **Circle** - Classic circular tokens
- 🐴 **Horse** - Fast and stylish
- 🦁 **Lion** - Powerful and bold
- 🐺 **Wolf** - Wild and fierce
- 💣 **Bomb** - Explosive moves
- ⭐ **Star** - Bright and shiny
- ❤️ **Heart** - Full of love

**You choose, we display!** Your selected shape appears on all your tokens.

### 2. **Dice in Your Corner**
No more searching for the dice at the bottom:
- **Red Player**: Dice in top-left corner
- **Green Player**: Dice in top-right corner
- **Blue Player**: Dice in bottom-right corner
- **Yellow Player**: Dice in bottom-left corner

### 3. **Extra Turns Built In**
You automatically get an extra turn when:
✅ You roll a **6**
✅ You **catch an enemy token**
✅ You reach **home** with a token

### 4. **Home Stretch - 6 Squares**
Each player has exactly 6 squares in their home stretch:
- Move from main track to home stretch
- Then move through 6 home stretch squares
- Finally reach the safe home position and WIN! 🏆

### 5. **Full Screen Support**
Works perfectly on:
- 📱 Mobile phones
- 📊 Tablets  
- 💻 Desktop screens

The board scales to fit your screen while maintaining square shape.

## 🎮 How to Play

### Starting a Game

1. **Click on game mode**
   - "Play vs AI" - You vs Computer
   - "Local Multiplayer" - You vs Friends

2. **Select number of players**
   - 2, 3, or 4 players

3. **Set AI difficulty** (if playing vs AI)
   - Easy, Medium, or Hard

4. **Choose your token shape** ⭐ NEW!
   - Pick from 7 available shapes
   - Your tokens will use this shape on the board

5. **Start Game** 🎲

### Playing the Game

1. **Roll the Dice**
   - Click the dice button in YOUR corner
   - Wait for your roll

2. **Move a Token**
   - See highlighted cells (valid moves)
   - Click any of your tokens to move
   - It moves to the highlighted cell

3. **Special Moves**
   - **Get a 6**: Extra turn! Roll again
   - **Catch Enemy**: Extra turn! Roll again
   - **Reach Home**: Extra turn! Roll again

4. **Win**
   - Get all 4 tokens to the home position (57)
   - Beat all other players
   - 🏆 See the victory screen!

### Track System
- **Starting Position**: Home area (your quarter)
- **Main Track**: 52 squares arranged around the board
- **Home Stretch**: 6 squares leading to your home corner
- **Safe Home**: Final position (won!)

### Safe Positions
Positions where you're safe from being caught:
⭐ Mark the safe positions where enemy tokens can't catch you

### Dice Locations in Corners
- Each player's dice is in their corner
- When it's your turn, your dice lights up
- Click to roll

## 🎯 Strategy Tips

1. **Bring Tokens Out**
   - You need a 6 to bring a token from home
   - Plan your first move carefully

2. **Move Strategically**
   - Don't put tokens on unprotected positions
   - Use safe positions when possible
   - Catch enemy tokens for extra turns

3. **Rush Home**
   - Once in home stretch, move tokens home quickly
   - Each token home = one step closer to winning

4. **Block Opponents**
   - Two tokens on same cell block opponents
   - Use this to your advantage

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I change my token shape mid-game?**
A: No, choose at the start. Play again if you want a different shape.

**Q: Do token shapes affect gameplay?**
A: No! They're purely visual. All tokens move the same.

**Q: Where's the dice?**
A: In your corner! Look for it in the corner matching your color.

**Q: How many squares in home stretch?**
A: Exactly 6 squares for every player.

**Q: Do I always get extra turn with 6?**
A: Yes! Rolling 6, catching enemy, or reaching home all give extra turn.
